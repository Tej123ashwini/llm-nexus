1. Zero-Shot Prompting
   - Giving a task directly to AI without any example.
   - No examples are given
   - Just ask the question directly

Example:  Translate ‘Good Morning’ into Hindi

2. Few-Shot Prompting
   - Giving a few examples before asking the actual question.

Example: 

Cat → Animal
Rose → Flower
Mango → ?

AI learns the pattern from examples and answers : Fruit


3. Chain of Thought (CoT) Prompting
  - Asking AI to think step-by-step before answering.

Example: If one pen costs 10 rupees, what is the cost of 5 pens? Explain step by step.

Cost of 1 pen = 10
Number of pens = 5
10 × 5 = 50

Answer:50 rupees

4. Role-Based Prompting
  - Giving AI a specific role or character before asking something.

Example: Act as a Java teacher and explain loops.


5. Prompting Without Role and Specific Details
  - Giving very little information or unclear instructions.
   - AI may give a general answer that may not match what you want.

Example: Explain programming.
